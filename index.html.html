📘 How to download your CUET Answer Key & Response Sheet PDF

1. Answer Key PDF

  1. Visit the official website: cuet.nta.nic.in
  2. Click on "Challenge(s) regarding Answer Key."
  3. Login using your Application Number and Password.
  4. Enter the captcha shown on the screen and click Submit.
  5. Click on "View Question Paper" to check marked/recorded responses.
  6. To view or challenge the answer key, click "Click to View/Challenge Answer Key."
  7. Select the required paper.
  8. Press Ctrl + P or click Print.
  9. Choose Save as PDF as the printer option.
  10. Set the layout to Landscape.
  11. Click Save to download the Answer Key PDF.

2. Response Sheet PDF

  1. Open the Response Sheet / View Question Paper page.
  2. Click on Print or press Ctrl + P.
  3. Select Save as PDF.
  4. Choose the required layout, preferably Landscape.
  5. Click Save to download the Response Sheet PDF.
